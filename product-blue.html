Welcome to Summit, a Clean One Page Theme.
Responsive. Customizable. Clean.

Customizable.

Each section is clearly defined in Summit meaning you can easily mix and match to create your perfect website. Because Summit is a one page theme, all you have to do is rearrange the sections in one page.

Responsive.

What does responsive mean? It means that Studio will adapt and conform to any device size and will still look great. Studio is great for those looking to capture a large mobile audience.

Crisp.
